about us

Hotshot is a Lebanese company founded by brothers Bchara and Rizk Chammas. Like other Lebanese citizens, they had enough of the lack of ready to use shots and realized that to get an alcohol buzz in any night out, you will need a really high budget. That’s how the idea of Hotshot came to life!

As one flavor didn’t seem enough, they created a long line of authentic and magical flavours: from Bubblegum and Tequila, to Sambuca and many others to satisfy all different tastes...

All the products are ready to drink, whereas you can just peel off the label and enjoy an everlasting tingle in your taste buds

Hotshot is there to provide you with exceptional tastes, and the unforgettable moments!
